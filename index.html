“The most courageous act is still to think for yourself. Aloud.”

“Think for yourself” means your thoughts should be your own—not what someone else has said or told you to think. By adding the phrase “aloud” at the end, the quote means it is brave to have your own thoughts and to say them to others.

“It’s not about ideas. It’s about making ideas happen.”

It’s about” is a way to highlight the importance of something. For example, “It’s about doing a good job” means what’s important is to do a good job. “Make it happen” is an expression that means to get things done or to do the work. For example, “If you want to get a promotion, then just make it happen” means if getting a promotion is your goal, then you must work now to get a promotion.
